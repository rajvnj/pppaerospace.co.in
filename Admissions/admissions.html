Welcome to Podar Prep Plus Preschool - Aerospace Park

Where Learning Begins with Joy and Curiosity and your child’s educational journey starts in a warm, safe, and stimulating environment, right in the heart of Podar Plus Aerospace Park.

Searching for the best preschool in Podar Plus Aerospace Park? You’ve found it.

At Podar Prep Plus, our thoughtfully designed programs cater to every stage of early childhood. We offer the following sections :

Grade LevelAge Requirement for AY 26-27

Playgroup (Seeds) 2 years complete by 1st June 2026

Our curriculum is led by certified educators and built around our pioneering ‘KIDUCATION’ philosophy—education from the child’s point of view. This unique approach ensures that every experience is age-appropriate, engaging, and culturally meaningful, helping children grow with confidence and joy.

Our Key distinguishing featureS

Our Glocal Curriculum blends the best of NEP 2020, EYFS (UK), and EYLF (Australia) to offer a holistic, globally-aligned learning experience that supports every aspect of a child's development.

We embrace a S.T.R.E.A.M.-based learning approach, enriched with storytelling and a SPIRAL curriculum design. This allows us to revisit and deepen key concepts throughout the year—making learning engaging, memorable, and built to last a lifetime.

Our 'Symphonics' program aligns with the goals of NIPUN Bharat, combining Glenn Doman techniques with whole language development to build strong early literacy skills.

The 'Number Magic Numeracy Education Program' transforms early math learning through a multisensory, research-based approach that makes numeracy engaging, intuitive, and lasting.

Our non-screen Coding Program uses Froebel Gifts, hands-on toys, and interactive games to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and human intelligence—preparing children for an AI-enabled future without relying on screens.

Direct Admission from Podar Prep to Grade 1. Podar Prep students receive direct admission to Grade 1 at Podar International School, ensuring a smooth transition and peace of mind for parents.

At Podar Prep, teaching goes beyond traditional methods. Our educators use high-impact, evidence-based strategies to support each child’s learning journey. All teachers and support staff receive rigorous training to ensure they meet every child’s developmental needs with care and expertise.

A dedicated space called the Sensory Spa allows children to engage all eight senses—including vestibular, proprioceptive, and interoceptive—supporting holistic brain and body development through calming, sensory-rich experiences.

FAQ's

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply for your child’s admission at Podar Prep online or by visiting the school. Our Headmistress at the centre will guide you through the entire process and answer all your questions regarding admissions.

What curriculum do you follow?

The KIDUCATION curriculum at Podar Prep is powered by NEP 2020 and boosted by EYFS (UK) and EYLF (Australia), that leads to holistic growth in children. It makes them independent, curious, and active learners. Through this robust GLOCAL practice of early childhood educators and philosophers from all across the world, we offer children a S.T.R.E.A.M. curriculum that is STORY-BASED and SPIRAL so that children learn through fun, and learning that stays for life.

What are the safety standards followed at Podar Prep Plus?

At Podar Prep, your child’s safety is our top priority. We take a comprehensive approach to ensure a secure and nurturing environment:

  • Regular safety discussions using age-appropriate PPTs and videos on topics like ‘Pappu the School Bus Safety’, ‘The Hand Family’, ‘Good Touch, Bad Touch’, and more.
  • Fire safety mock drills conducted regularly.
  • CCTV surveillance across classrooms, common areas, and school buses.
  • Finger guards on doors to prevent accidental injuries.
  • Emergency provisions in place, including first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and alarm systems.
  • Detailed student records maintained, including ‘All About Me’ forms and allergy lists.
  • All staff strictly follow a zero-tolerance policy for any breach of safety protocols.

Safety isn’t just a measure—it’s a mindset we practice every day.

Do you have transportation facilities?

Yes, Podar Prep provides safe transportation for children, with GPS tracking on the school buses to keep parents informed and CCTV camera installation.

What are the health and safety protocols in place?

To keep all our kids and staff protected, we have protocols that include:

  • Jumbo Snack Menu for building healthy eating habits
  • Birthday Policy with no cakes and sweets in celebrations at school
  • Parent Smart Card
  • Child-Friendly Furniture
  • Low commodes and wash basins in the washrooms
  • Visual reminders for washing hands and flushing the toilet
  • Installation of finger guards on all classroom doors
  • Panic alarm in the washroom
  • Mock Fire Drills
  • Good Touch and Bad Touch Muppet Video
  • First Aid Kits
  • Tie Up with the Nearby Hospital in case of emergency
